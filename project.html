Project Summary

This corporate office project was delivered as a full turnkey scope—starting from space replanning and concept development through design approvals and execution. The goal was to optimize flow, improve functional zoning, and deliver a refined contemporary workspace aligned with operational needs.

Key Deliverables

  • Space replanning and functional zoning
  • Design development and authority approvals
  • Turnkey execution and site coordination
  • Quality control, detailing, and final handover
